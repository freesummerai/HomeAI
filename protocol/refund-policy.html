Understanding the Billing Cycle

Your Revoom subscription is as determined, and will be billed and automatically renewed 24 hours before the end of the cycle. Please note that your subscription can be cancelled at any time under your iPhone/iPad by going to Settings > Apple ID Profile > Subscriptions.

You will not be able to receive a refund for subscription plan that has auto-renewed. However, your subscription access and/or delivery and accompanying subscriber benefits will continue for the remainder of the current billing period, after your termination of the subscription.
